隐私政策

生效日期：2026年3月19日

感谢您使用「禅择 ZenChoice」（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

1. 信息收集

本应用收集的信息极为有限：

  • 用户输入内容：您在应用中输入的"想做的事"文本。此内容仅用于生成鼓励内容。
  • 订阅状态：通过 Apple StoreKit 框架管理，我们不直接收集或存储您的支付信息。
  • 语言偏好与自定义视角设置：存储在您设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。

2. 数据使用

  • 本地存储：您的勇气档案记录、语言设置、自定义视角配置均存储在设备本地（UserDefaults），不会同步到任何云端服务器。
  • AI 生成服务（仅订阅用户）：当您使用自定义视角功能时，您输入的文本会发送至第三方 AI 服务（阿里云通义千问/DashScope）用于生成个性化鼓励内容。该服务仅处理文本内容，不会关联您的个人身份信息。

3. 第三方服务

  • Apple StoreKit：用于处理应用内订阅购买，受 Apple 隐私政策约束。
  • 阿里云 DashScope API：仅订阅用户的自定义视角功能使用，用于 AI 文本生成。我们仅发送您输入的文本内容，不发送任何设备标识或个人信息。

4. 数据存储与安全

所有用户数据均存储在您的设备本地。我们不运营任何用户数据服务器。卸载应用将删除所有本地数据。

5. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

6. 隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并更新生效日期。

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

Thank you for using ZenChoice. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect very limited information:

  • User Input: The text you enter describing what you want to do. This is used solely to generate encouragement content.
  • Subscription Status: Managed through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not directly collect or store your payment information.
  • Language Preferences & Custom Perspective Settings: Stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Local Storage: Your courage archive records, language settings, and custom perspective configurations are stored locally on your device (UserDefaults) and are not synced to any cloud server.
  • AI Generation Service (Subscribers Only): When you use the custom perspectives feature, your input text is sent to a third-party AI service (Alibaba Cloud Qwen/DashScope) to generate personalized encouragement. This service only processes the text content and does not associate it with your personal identity.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: Used to process in-app subscription purchases, governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Alibaba Cloud DashScope API: Used only for the custom perspectives feature (subscribers only) to generate AI text. We only send your input text — no device identifiers or personal information.

4. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any user data servers. Uninstalling the app will delete all local data.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
